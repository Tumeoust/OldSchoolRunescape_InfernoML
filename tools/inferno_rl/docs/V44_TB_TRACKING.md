# V44 TB Tracking

## Goal

Train from scratch with three structural upgrades: factored action space, privileged critic observations, and
schedule-driven reward shaping decay. V44 is a full reset — no warmstart from V43.

## Base Checkpoint

Fresh from scratch (no warmstart).

## Why V44 Exists

V43 peaked at 28% W55-66 clear rate (ckpt_12000, ~144M steps) and a return_mean high of 3.41 at 141.8M. Training was
stopped at ~153.7M for three compounding reasons:

1. **Shaping drift.** Dense shaping terms (Single-LOS, NE Pillar Zone, C Tile, Tile A, Mager Priority) had fixed
   magnitudes throughout training. Early on this was necessary for credit assignment, but by 100M+ the agent was
   optimizing shaping instead of progressing. The `shape_share` metric and repeated RVar climbs confirmed this.

2. **Flat action space limitations.** The Discrete(43) action space forced the policy to learn correlations between
   unrelated action types (movement, attack, switch) in a single softmax. This made exploration inefficient and
   gradient signal noisy — a movement exploration step would perturb attack probabilities and vice versa.

3. **Value function accuracy plateau.** The critic saw the same 317 dims as the actor, missing information it could
   use for better value estimation (exact NPC cooldowns, blob scan forecasts, overflow counts). EV oscillated between
   .88-.97 and VL never stabilized below .03 for sustained periods.

## What Changed (V43 -> V44)

### 1. Factored action space

Legacy `Discrete(43)` replaced with `MultiDiscrete([5, 32, 5, 4])` = 46 mask entries.

| Head     | Size | Values                                             |
|----------|------|----------------------------------------------------|
| mode     | 5    | stay, move, attack, switch, noop                   |
| movement | 32   | 8 directions × 4 distances (legacy actions 1-32)   |
| attack   | 5    | priority 1-3, nearest nibbler, barrage (33-37)     |
| switch   | 4    | BoFa, blowpipe, ice barrage, blood barrage (39-42) |

Head dependencies: movement/attack/switch heads are gated by mode selection. If `mode != move`, the movement head is
masked entirely (and similarly for attack/switch).

Bidirectional `encode_policy_action` / `decode_policy_action` bridges to the legacy simulator action space.

### 2. Privileged critic observations

Actor sees public observation (317 dims, unchanged from V3.2). Critic sees full observation (363 dims = 317 + 46
privileged).

| Block                      | Dims | Description                                                    |
|----------------------------|------|----------------------------------------------------------------|
| Per-slot extras            | 36   | 6 types × 2 slots × 3 features (attack_delay, stunned, frozen) |
| Blob scan trigger forecast | 8    | Per 8 movement directions                                      |
| Overflow                   | 2    | Overflow entity count + nearest overflow distance              |

The per-slot extras provide the critic with continuous NPC cooldown/state values (the actor only sees binary
stunned/frozen flags and clamped delay). The blob scan triggers let the critic anticipate prayer-reveal events.
Overflow features handle entities beyond the 2-per-type slot limit.

### 3. Reward scheduling

`RewardConfig` dataclass with 9 tunable shaping fields, driven by `build_v44_reward_config(trained_rollouts)`.

Schedule types: `ConstantSchedule`, `LinearSchedule`, `PiecewiseSchedule`.

| Reward term              | Schedule  | Start | End    | Duration      |
|--------------------------|-----------|-------|--------|---------------|
| `tile_a_max_reward`      | Linear    | 0.04  | 0.0    | 500 rollouts  |
| `single_los_engagement`  | Linear    | 0.02  | 0.005  | 2000 rollouts |
| `ne_pillar_zone_bonus`   | Linear    | 0.008 | 0.002  | 2000 rollouts |
| `ne_pillar_zone_penalty` | Linear    | -0.02 | -0.005 | 2000 rollouts |
| `mager_priority`         | Piecewise | 0.25  | 0.125  | at 4000       |
| `c_tile_on`              | Linear    | 0.5   | 0.0    | 500 rollouts  |
| `c_tile_adjacent`        | Linear    | 0.25  | 0.0    | 500 rollouts  |
| `avoidable_los_penalty`  | Constant  | -0.02 | —      | —             |
| `avoidable_imminent`     | Constant  | -0.01 | —      | —             |

Design rationale: early-game crutches (Tile A, C Tile positioning) decay fastest (500 rollouts). Engagement shaping
decays over 2000 rollouts to 25% of initial magnitude — enough for credit assignment, not enough to farm. Kill order
shaping (mager priority) halves at 4000 rollouts. Avoidable-LOS penalties are constant since they represent genuine
mistakes at any training stage.

#### Novelty reward schedule

Observation-space novelty bonus (distributional outlier detection): `0.0003 → 0.0` linear over 300 rollouts. Encourages
early exploration when starting from scratch, vanishes before it can distort shaping. Inspired by Naton1 PvP RL
(`0.00025 → 0.0` over 200 rollouts). Implemented via `_V44_NOVELTY_SCHEDULE` in `train_gpu.py`.

### 4. Training infrastructure

**TrainingChampionTracker**: guardrail-based auto-revert after 3 consecutive failures.

Guardrails:

- EV >= 0.90
- value_loss <= 0.08
- KL >= 0.0015
- grad_norm <= 2.5
- shape_share <= 1.0

Score = weighted sweep score: `0.7 * normalized_progress + 0.3 * success_rate`, weighted 2x for waves >= 55.

On 3 consecutive guardrail failures: reloads best champion checkpoint (preserving wall-clock step/rollout counters).

## V44 Hypotheses

1. **Factored action space** should improve exploration efficiency and reduce gradient interference between action
   types. Expect faster early learning and cleaner policy updates (higher KL per update, lower clip fraction).

2. **Privileged critic** should reduce value estimation error. Expect lower VL and higher EV earlier in training
   compared to V43, and less EV oscillation.

3. **Reward scheduling** should prevent shaping drift. Expect `shape_share` to decline over training rather than
   climbing. The `running_reward_var` should grow more slowly than V43.

4. **Champion tracker** should prevent catastrophic regressions. If the policy enters a bad region (VL spike,
   grad_norm explosion, KL collapse), it should auto-revert rather than continuing to deteriorate.

5. Combined: V44 should reach V43's 28% W55-66 clear rate in fewer steps, and continue improving past the ~150M
   plateau.

### Continuation — n_epochs bump (ckpt 300, ~4.9M steps)

KL collapsed to 0.0003 by ~5.8M steps (66× below target). Root cause: small normalized advantages
(well-fitted critic) × single epoch = near-zero actor gradient. Fix: n_epochs 1→2. Off-policy bias
is negligible while KL is this low. target_kl=0.02 early stop provides the safety valve.

### Continuation — gamma bump (ready to run at ~68M steps)

OpenAI Five's strongest finding: increasing gamma from 0.9993 → 0.9997 → 0.9998 steadily improved strategic play.
Current V44 gamma=0.995 gives horizon ~200 ticks (~27s). Inferno episodes run 100-250 ticks (60-150s), so our
horizon covers less than half the typical episode. Bumping to gamma=0.997 (horizon ~333 ticks, ~45s) — a moderate
step rather than jumping straight to 0.998. Monitor for VL spike and KL collapse — if the critic can't fit the
longer horizon, revert.

### Continuation — LOS reward fade (~74.9M steps, 2026-03-16)

`Avoidable_LOS` and `Single-LOS_Engagement` faded to 0 over 300 rollouts starting at rollout ~4570. Both terms were
constraining late-wave optimal play: the penalty punished tactically necessary LOS exposure (chasing magers, navigating
pillars), and the single-LOS bonus was nearly unachievable on later waves, nudging toward passivity. The fade is
smooth (LinearSchedule from current values → 0.0). `avoidable_imminent_penalty` is unchanged — that penalizes specific
imminent-attack LOS which is always a mistake. See `_V44_LOS_REMOVAL_START` / `_V44_LOS_FADE_DURATION` in `rewards.py`.

### Continuation — Curriculum narrowing + grad_norm bump (~107M steps, 2026-03-17)

100-seed deterministic eval (W49→66) at ckpt_1700 (~103.2M) showed 81% clear rate — up from 51.6% at ckpt_3800
(~67.2M). But 76% of deaths cluster in W60-63: the agent handles W49-59 near-perfectly but struggles with the final
wave compositions. Full sweep W1-66 wastes ~75% of compute on solved waves.

Two changes:

1. **Curriculum narrowing** (`--start-wave 49`): Concentrates all rollout time on the failure region. Reduces RVar
   (eliminates easy/hard bimodality) and gives the critic a focused value landscape. The agent already handles W1-48
   with near-zero failure rate.

2. **max_grad_norm bump** (`--max-grad-norm 1.0`, was 0.5): grad_norm has been consistently clipping at 0.5-0.95
   throughout the 68M-107M range, limiting both actor and critic updates. The recurring EV/VL oscillation cycle
   (good state → large clip_frac → VL spike → recovery) correlates with clipping. OpenAI Five used 5.0; 1.0 is a
   conservative step. Champion tracker guardrail (grad_norm <= 2.5) still catches real divergence.

Gamma held at 0.997 — RVar creep (19.2→23.1 over 68M-107M) suggests the critic is already struggling with the
current horizon. Gamma bump to 0.998 deferred until RVar stabilizes post-curriculum change.

### Continuation — LR reduction + vf_coef reduction (~115.6M steps, 2026-03-17)

Recurring EV/VL oscillation cycle throughout the w49-66 segment (3 crises in ~6M steps at ~112M, ~113M, ~115M).
Root cause analysis: KL is consistently 0.001–0.002 (well below target_kl=0.02) — the **actor** barely moves each
update. The VL spikes are critic-driven, not actor-driven. The critic repeatedly scrambles to refit value estimates
for hard late-wave states after each moderate policy step. Running_reward_mean has been flat at 9.05–9.06 for the
entire segment — no progress.

Two changes:

1. **LR reduction** (`--lr 1e-4`, was 3e-4): At 115M+ steps, 3e-4 is on the high end; scheduled decay is overdue.
   Smaller steps reduce the magnitude of critic updates that cause EV/VL oscillations.

2. **vf_coef reduction** (`--vf-coef 0.3`, was 0.5): The critic loss dominates the gradient signal (PGL ≈ -0.004,
   VL ≈ 0.07 × 0.5 = 0.035). Reducing vf_coef directly reduces critic contribution to grad_norm without slowing
   the actor. More targeted than LR alone.

Gamma held at 0.997, curriculum held at w49-66, max-grad-norm held at 1.0.

## Reward-Term Watchlist

Track these from the start:

- `Single-LOS Engagement` — should decay from 0.02 to 0.005 over 2000 rollouts
- `NE Pillar Zone` / `NE Pillar Zone Penalty` — should decay over 2000 rollouts
- `C Tile Position` — should reach 0 by rollout 500
- `Tile A Proximity` — should reach 0 by rollout 500
- `Mager Priority` — should halve at rollout 4000
- `shape_share` (champion tracker) — should trend downward, not upward
- `Stall Penalty` — should not explode (V43 146.5M alarm pattern)
- `novelty_mean` / `novelty_reward_scale` — should decay to 0 by rollout 300
- `Wave Complete` / `Damage Dealt` — progress terms should grow as shaping decays

Red flags:

- `shape_share` > 0.5 after 1000 rollouts (shaping not decaying as expected)
- `running_reward_var` sustained climb exceeding V43 rate
- positive shaping terms climbing while kills/completions stagnate
- guardrail auto-revert firing repeatedly (champion tracker stuck)

## Success Criteria

1. Reach V43 peak (28% W55-66) in fewer total steps.
2. Continue improving past 150M (V43 plateau region).
3. `shape_share` declines over training.
4. `value_loss` and `running_reward_var` bounded better than V43.
5. Champion tracker auto-revert fires infrequently (< 5% of checkpoints).

## Failure / Stop Criteria

1. V41-style divergence: `value_loss`, `return_mean`, and `running_reward_var` all accelerating together.
2. Factored action space produces degenerate mode distribution (one mode > 90% probability).
3. Champion tracker stuck in revert loop (> 10 consecutive guardrail failures).
4. No improvement over V43 baseline by 100M steps.
